Process

How the Application Works

Four straightforward steps. No fees. Approximately 30 minutes with documents ready.

01

Review Eligibility

Check citizenship, enrollment status, and SSN requirements before you begin your application.

02

Complete the Form

Fill out the secure 5-step application with your personal, contact, and financial information.

03

Review & Submit

Verify all entries on the summary page, then submit with your digital certification.

04

Receive Your Report

Your Student Aid Report (SAR) arrives by email within 3–5 business days of submission.

Aid Program Description Repayment? Maximum Award

Direct Subsidized LoansLoan Need-based. Government covers interest while enrolled at least half-time. Yes $5,500 / year
